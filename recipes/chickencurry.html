buttery chicken coconut curry (back to recipes)

Description

this curry recipe combines the spiciness of an indian curry with the richness of thai coconut curry.

adding coconut milk towards the end of cooking results in a stronger coconut flavor in the dish

Ingredients

for the curry

  • 800g crushed tomatoes approx. 2 cups
  • 300g coconut milk not coconut cream!
  • one large or two medium onions, any kind
  • your favorite vegetables suggestions:paprika, potatoes, spinach, pumpkin, beans, etc
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • your favorite chili pepper or hot sauce
  • 600g chicken thighs

spices (add at the end of cooking)

  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala

How to make

  1. sear both sides of the chicken, remove from pan and add the onions and start to brown them
  2. when the onions are almost browned, add grated ginger and garlic, cook for another min
  3. add vegetables of your choice and cook them slightly (2-3 min)
  4. add the crushed tomatoes, bay leaves, black pepper, paprika powder, salt and chili peppers. we will let this simmer on low heat for ~20 minutes
    • chop up the chicken thighs to your desired size and add them to the curry as well
  5. when there is 5 minutes left of cooking, pour in the coconut milk and stir well
  6. once the 20 minutes have passed, add the curry powder and garam masala. we add these at the end because they develop a bitter taste when heated too much
  7. done! enjoy with rice or ramen noodles, or with naan bread!
