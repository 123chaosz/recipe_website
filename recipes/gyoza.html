crispy potstickers (gyoza) (薄皮餃子) (back to recipes)

Description

ground pork, cabbage, and green onion wrapped in a thin, round piece of dough.

the wrapped meat can be cooked in a frying pan or steamed.

Ingredients

makes:50 gyoza
  • 400g ground pork
  • 3-4 cabbage leaves
    • you will need ice for blanching the cabbage
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 50 gyoza wrappers

prepare the gyoza

  1. boil the cabbage leaves for 1 min then put them in a bowl of ice cold water. this process will soften the leaves and the cold water will keep them from being mushy
  2. chop the green onions very finely and grate the ginger
  3. add the ground pork and (1) and (2) into a bowl. add the rest of the ingredients and proceed to knead the meat with your hands.
  4. once everything has been completely mixed together. ready a bowl of water and the gyoza wrappers
  5. put the wrapper on the gyoza press and put about a tsp of the meat in the middle. spread the meat out so it becomes like a flat circle. then dab a little water on the outer edge of the wrapper and close the press.

cooking the gyoza

  1. preheat a hot plate or carbon steel pan and then add cooking oil
  2. place the gyoza in the pan and cook until the bottom is seared
  3. add around 100-150ml of water to the pan and place a lid on the pan. cook until the water has evaporated and the starch from the wrappers has crisped and turned a golden brown color.
  4. put on a plate and enjoy! dipping sauce:1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp rice vinegar, 1/4 tsp la-yu (chili oil)
